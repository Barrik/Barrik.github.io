My Sites

I am at the beginning of the process of learning to code. I'm nearly finished with my second 6-week course at Geekwise Academy. This list of sites not only represents what I have learned throughout my time there, but also the process of my learning. From simple to beautiful, these websites aren't every piece of work I've ever done: they're what I'm proud of, and what I feel to be up to my standards. Over time, this list will be updated, as I learn new things and put them to use on new projects.

This portfolio site itself is a work of art that I am very proud of. While I kept it simple, to keep the focus on what is being displayed, I put a lot of time and effort into making a unique design and interface that I did not know how to make before this.

Bootcamp 1

This was my first ever website, done in my first coding class. This is where it all started. You might notice a lot of themes from this site that have carried over into my newer work. Notice the similar color scheme and basic layout.

Pet Store

This website was a sketch project in Bootcamp 2. We were given this and this image, and told to recreate it.
